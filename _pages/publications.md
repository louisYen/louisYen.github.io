</div>

<div class="publication-card pub-adaptation">

  <div class="publication-content">

    <div class="publication-venue">AAAI 2026</div>

    <h3 class="publication-title">
      Alfa: Attentive Low-Rank Filter Adaptation for Structure-Aware Cross-Domain Personalized Gaze Estimation
    </h3>

    <p class="publication-authors">
      <strong>He-Yen Hsieh</strong>, Wei-Te Mark Ting, H. T. Kung
    </p>

    <p class="publication-desc">
      Alfa adapts gaze models to new users and domains with only a few unlabeled samples by reweighting useful spatial patterns already learned in pre-trained filters. The same low-rank adaptation idea also extends to diffusion-based language models for reasoning tasks.
    </p>

    <div class="publication-tags">
      Efficient adaptation · Cross-domain personalization · Gaze estimation · PEFT/LoRA · Model compression
